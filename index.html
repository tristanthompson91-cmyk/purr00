02 — How It Works

Four steps
to a full look.

One natural language prompt. Two data pipelines. One complete, shoppable outfit — in seconds.

01
Customer Prompt
Free-form natural language — occasion, vibe, style icons, weather, body preferences. No dropdowns. No filters. Just talk.
02
Style Scan
PURR indexes Pinterest boards, Instagram aesthetics, editorial lookbooks and street style — sourcing the visual DNA of the request.
03
Inventory Match
Hard-locked to the retailer's live catalog. Only in-stock SKUs. Only real prices. PURR drives conversions to the partner — not a competitor.
04
Outfit Output
A styled, shoppable outfit with a stylist note. Swap any piece and the rest of the look updates to stay cohesive.

05 — Business Model

Three ways
PURR makes money.

Revenue on both sides — from retailers who embed PURR, and from shoppers who love it.

01
B2B — Retailer SaaS
Monthly License Fee
Fashion Nova, PLT, Shein — any retailer with a large catalog pays to embed PURR as a white-label widget. Tiered by MAU and SKU count.
  • $2,000–$15,000 per month per retailer
  • Custom branding and widget theming
  • API + plug-and-play widget deployment
02
B2B — Revenue Share
Per-Sale Commission
Affiliate commission on every outfit that converts to a purchase through PURR. We win when the retailer wins.
  • 3–8% of attributed GMV
  • Tracked via session ID + UTM
  • No sale = no charge
03
B2C — Consumer App
PURR Pro Subscription
Freemium model. Free tier: 3 outfit generations per month. Pro unlocks unlimited styling, digital wardrobe, and style memory.
  • $9.99/month for Pro
  • Unlimited outfit generations
  • Digital wardrobe + style memory
04
Enterprise — White-Label API
License the Engine
License PURR's underlying AI to subscription boxes, stylists, or brands who want the intelligence without building it.
  • Enterprise pricing
  • Full API access + custom fine-tuning
  • Scales to any catalog size

1. Who We Are

PURR is an AI-powered personal styling platform operated by Purr, Inc. We provide AI-driven outfit recommendations through our web application and integrations with retail partners. Contact: hello@purr.style

2. Information We Collect
  • Styling prompts and preferences — the text you enter when requesting outfit recommendations.
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, interaction patterns within the application.
  • Device and browser information — browser type, OS, IP address, general geographic location.
  • Account information (if applicable) — name, email, and saved wardrobe preferences.
3. Pinterest Data

PURR integrates with the Pinterest API to access publicly available pin imagery and style inspiration data solely to improve outfit recommendation relevance. We do not store, redistribute, or sell Pinterest content. We comply fully with Pinterest's API Terms of Service and Developer Guidelines. We do not access your personal Pinterest account without explicit authorization.

4. How We Use Your Information
  • To generate personalized outfit recommendations based on your styling prompts
  • To improve the accuracy and relevance of our AI styling model
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the PURR platform
  • To communicate with you about your account or service updates
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data to train AI models without your explicit consent.

5. Data Sharing
  • Retail partners — anonymized, aggregated styling trend data only. No personally identifiable information.
  • Service providers — trusted third-party services processing data under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
6. Data Retention

We retain styling prompts and usage data for up to 12 months. Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. Request deletion any time at hello@purr.style. We process deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Your Rights
  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of data processing for marketing purposes
  • Data portability (California residents under CCPA; EU residents under GDPR)
8. Cookies

PURR uses minimal cookies necessary for the application to function. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. We do not track you across other websites.

9. Children's Privacy

PURR is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Contact hello@purr.style if you believe a child has provided us personal data.
